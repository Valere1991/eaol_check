# Sector Strategy

EAOL is horizontal, but adoption should start with sector packs. A sector pack defines common systems, entities, anomalies, connectors, and compliance needs.

## Initial sector packs

1. Génie civil / Construction
2. Finance / Banque / Assurance
3. IT / Cloud / Support
4. Mécanique / Manufacturing
5. Secteur public / Collectivités

## Expansion sectors

- Logistics and supply chain
- Energy and utilities
- Healthcare and life sciences
- Retail and e-commerce
- Telecom
- Legal and professional services

## Why sector packs matter

They make a horizontal platform sellable:

- faster demos
- clearer ROI
- prebuilt vocabulary
- prebuilt connectors
- prebuilt graph templates
- compliance story per industry
